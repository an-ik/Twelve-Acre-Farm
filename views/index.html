Field trips

By appointment only

Spend an hour or two at the farm with your own group.

  • Customize your experience according to age and group goals (badge earning, recreation, curriculum requirements, sensitivity to special needs, etc.)

Calling all volunteers!

The farm is always on the look out for volunteers. Please contact us if you are interested in horse handling and/or farm chores.

To sign up, please email Dawn at dawn@twelveacrefarm.com

Farm chores

Help with chores around the farm and gain the benefits from caring for the animals.

  • You will be trained on various chores such as manure mucking and clean water maintenance
  • You may also share any skills you may have such as building/fixing structures, weeding, etc.
  • Come on your own or with friends
  • Adult supervision required at all times

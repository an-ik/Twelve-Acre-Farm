Spring/Summer Programs

Twelve Acre Farms is offering a variety of programs in the spring and summer of 2021.

  • April 6th - June 12th
  • July 12th - 16th
  • July 19th - 23rd
  • July 26th - 30th

Registration/liability forms are required for all activities. Six-foot social distancing is required for all outside activities. Masks are recommended for close contact.

After School Program

Tuesdays & Thursdays (4pm - 6pm)

10-week program; 1 day/week (Tuesday or Thursday)

  • Gardening and petting zoo activities
  • Transportation required
  • $300 (no refunds/open rain or shine)

Pre-K story hour

Tuesdays (10am - 11am)

Hear some fun farm stories and visit the animals in the petting zoo.

  • Adult supervision required at all times
  • Suggested donation of $5 per child per hour

Horse Lessons

By appointment only

Book a private lesson to learn horesmanship and or riding (beginner or therapeutic).

  • Evaluation required to ensure safety and appropriate horse availability
  • $40/half hour or $60/full hour

Private group time

By appointment only

Spend an hour or two at the farm with your own group.

  • Customize your experience according to age and group goals (badge earning, recreation, curriculum requirements, sensitivity to special needs, etc.)
  • Adult supervision is required at all times
  • Suggested donation of $5 per participant per hour (flexible/no charge for supervising adults)

Calling all Volunteers!

The farm is always on the look out for volunteers. Please contact us if you are interested in horse handling and/or farm chores.

Horse Handling

Help train rescued horses for new jobs interacting with humans. No experience required.

  • Must be at least 14 years old to participate
  • Parental permission is required
  • Come on your own or with friends
  • You will be trained to care for animals and to track, lead, and sidewalk during lessons

Farm Chores

Help with chores around the farm and gain the benefits from caring for the animals.

  • You will be trained on various chores such as manure mucking and clean water maintenance
  • You may also share any skills you may have such as building/fixing structures, weeding, etc.
  • Come on your own or with friends
  • Adult supervision required at all times
